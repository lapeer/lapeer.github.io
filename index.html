about me:

i am a first year astrophysics phd student at the university of massachusetts amherst

 

my general research interests are:

  • how galaxies evolve over cosmic time
  • co-evolution of galaxies and supermassive black holes
  • feedback in the galactic ecosystem

i am currently working with professor daniela calzetti on understanding the feedback produced by star clusters in the early stages of their life.
