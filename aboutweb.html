i picked up web design as a hobby mid-2024. since then, i have increasingly made an effort to center my online presence around my website. instead of social media, i now share things about myself here, in a realm of my own creation. i like to share my interests, artwork, thoughts, ideas, and research here! do note that this website is ever changing, i update it semi-regularly, and some of the pages may be under construction.


this site is hosted on my github, but my neocities page also redirects here. my domain is a reference to giant molecular clouds, which are complexes of cold molecular gas, and the primary site of star formation in the local universe. if you cannot tell, my career is a big part of my life, and i enjoy theming things around it. all of the code you see her is written by me, unless stated otherwise. i do not support the use of generative AI; all code on this site is written by humans. the template for this site was originally from the wonderful ribo.zone. i have made some major changes, but the bones are still there!
